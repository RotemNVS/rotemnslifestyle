Fashion = Passion. Fashion And Styling
Are Part From My Lifestyle

Lifestyle Fashion
  • Rotem Narder
  • 1 September 2018
  • 502
Share Tweet Pin Email

What does fashion mean to me? What does fashion trigger in me?

This time in fashion, I would like to share with you the passion and attraction I have for fashion.

So how did my love affair with fashion begin?

From a young age my grandmother sewed and designed dresses for me.

I really liked the fact she sewed and adjusted me dresses that were exclusively for me.

From the age of six I liked adding and matching accessories to my clothing.

I delayed leaving the house thinking what would I wear today and with what would I combine it, as early as I was six year old.

Since I was the six years old, I have loved modeling and posing in front of the camera.

At the age of 10 I got a muse and wanted to sew a dress for my Barbie. I remember taking a paper and starting to sketch on it the dress I was thinking about in my head. After sketching the design, I went to my parents' wardrobe and looked for raw materials from which I could create the dress. I used my father's white T-shirts, a needle and coloured sewing threads.

How does my love affair with fashion continue?

From the age of 14 I have been following trends in fashion, evaluating fashionistas and looking for the new item of clothing or the new accessory for my closet.

My outlook on people and on the environment is different, I naturally scan the environment and notice the total look. I scan the environment as an characterization of something fashionable and interesting - when the search query runs through what I'm exposed to in my daily life in shop windows, online stores, advertisements, people's clothing, clips, magazines and articles.

In the leisure time I like to watch Project Runway, The Next Top Model, the Fashion channel, the Fashion Police on channel E, to design fashion jewellery, and to give a styling advising to others.

So this is a story about my ongoing love affair with the fashion world.

Fashion = My Passion
